About Us

We are a group of engineering students with a vision to make our community a better place. Milan is like a dream to us. As the name suggests, ‘Milan’ is the colloquial term for the word ‘conjugation’. Here at Milan we aim at bringing all the NGOs together across the world at a platform which will help the people find their right community to serve hence our moto ‘where help meets need’.
